CONSERVE AND SUSTAINABLY USE THE OCEANS, SEAS AND MARINE RESOURCES FOR SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT

"Although the sound is inaudible above water, depending on where you are or the time of year, the underwater soundscape can be as noisy as any jungle or any forest" - Kate Stafford

⁙

Don't let the ocean's song be silenced by human noise. Don't let the real sound of the ocean be drowned out, bring back its natural song.

The ocean is full of natural sounds — from whales singing to waves crashing — that reflect its health and life. But the hum of human greed is drowning it out, and if we don't act, we might silence the ocean forever.
